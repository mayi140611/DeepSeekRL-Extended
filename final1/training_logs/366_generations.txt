###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Keaton climbed a 30 feet ladder twenty times while working at the construction site. Reece, also working at the same site, climbed a ladder 4 feet shorter than Keaton's ladder 15 times. What's the total length of the ladders that both workers climbed in inches?

system
Reasoning: To calculate the total length climbed by both workers, first calculate the total feet climbed by each worker separately. Keaton climbed a 30 feet ladder twenty times, so 30 feet * 20 = 600 feet. Reece's ladder is 4 feet shorter, so it is 30 feet - 4 feet = 26 feet. Reece climbed this ladder 15 times, so 26 feet * 15 = 390 feet. Combining both results, the total feet climbed by both workers is 600 feet + 390 feet = 990 feet.
