###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Earl started delivering newspapers on the first floor of a condominium building. He then went up 5 floors then went down 2 floors. He again went up 7 floors and found that he is 9 floors away from the top of the building. How many floors does the building have?

#### ANS ####

20
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
Let's determine the total number of floors Earl has climbed and then subtract the distance he is still away from the top to find the total number of floors in the building.

1. Initially, Earl starts on the first floor.
2. He goes up 5 floors: First floor + 5 floors = 6th floor.
3. He then goes down 2 floors: 6th floor - 2 floors = 4th floor.
4. He goes up 7 floors: 4th floor + 7 floors = 11th floor.

Earl is now 9 floors away from the top of the building. This means the 11th floor is 9 floors below the top of the building.

To find the total number of floors in the building, we add the 11th floor and the 9 floors:

11th floor + 9 floors = 20 floors

So, the building must have 20 floors.

</reasoning>
