###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Yuan is 7 years older than his brother David and twice his age. How old is David?

#### ANS ####

7
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
Let's denote David's age as \( D \).

According to the information given:
1. Yuan is 7 years older than David, so Yuan's age is \( D + 7 \).
2. Yuan is also twice David's age, so Yuan's age is \( 2D \).

We can set up the equation based on these two pieces of information:
\[ D + 7 = 2D \]

Now, we solve for \( D \):
\[ D + 7 = 2D \]
\[ 7 = 2D - D \]
\[ 7 = D \]

So, David is 7 years old.

Let's verify the solution with the given conditions:
- David's age is \( D = 7 \).
- Yuan's age is \( D + 7 = 7 + 7 = 14 \).
- Yuan's age is \( 2D = 2 \times 7 = 14 \).

Since both conditions are satisfied, the solution is correct.

</reasoning>
<answer>
7
</answer>
