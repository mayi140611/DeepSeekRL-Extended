###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

In a classroom, 10 children have 7 books each. Their teacher brings another 8 books to the classroom. How many books are in the classroom altogether?

#### ANS ####

78
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####


system
Initially, the 10 children have 7 books each, so they have a total of \(10 \times 7 = 70\) books.

The teacher brings an additional 8 books.

So, the total number of books in the classroom is \(70 + 8 = 78\).

</reasoning>
<answer>
78
</answer>
