###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Ten boys brought 15 popsicle sticks each. Twelve girls brought 12 popsicle sticks each. How many fewer popsicle sticks did the girls bring than the boys?

#### ANS ####

6
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####


system
<reasoning>
To determine how many fewer popsicle sticks the girls brought than the boys, we first need to calculate the total number of popsicle sticks brought by the boys and the girls separately.

The boys brought \(10 \times 15 = 150\) popsicle sticks each.
The girls brought \(12 \times 12 = 144\) popsicle sticks each.

The difference in the number of popsicle sticks brought by the girls and the boys is:
\(150 - 144 = 6\).

Therefore, the girls brought 6 fewer popsicle sticks than the boys.
</reasoning>
<answer>
6
</answer>
