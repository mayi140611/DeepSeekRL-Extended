###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

The flowers cost $9, the clay pot costs $20 more than the flower, and the bag of soil costs $2 less than the flower.  How much does it cost to plant the flowers?

#### ANS ####

45
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
To find the total cost for planting the flowers, we need to determine the cost of each item and sum them up.

1. The cost of the flowers is given as $9.
2. The cost of the clay pot is $20 more than the flowers. Therefore, the cost of the clay pot is \( 9 + 20 = $29\).
3. The cost of the bag of soil is $2 less than the flowers. Therefore, the cost of the bag of soil is \( 9 - 2 = $7\).

Now, let's sum up these costs to find the total cost:
- Flowers: $9
- Clay pot: $29
- Bag of soil: $7

Total cost = Flowers + Clay pot + Bag of soil
Total cost = $9 + $29 + $7
Total cost = $45

Therefore, the total cost to plant the flowers is $45.
</reasoning>
<answer>
