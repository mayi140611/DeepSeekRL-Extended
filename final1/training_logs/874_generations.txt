###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Santino has 2 papaya trees and 3 mango trees. If each papaya tree produces 10 papayas and each mango tree produces 20 mangos, how many fruits does Santino have in total?

#### ANS ####

80
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####


system
<reasoning>
To find out the total number of fruits Santino has, we need to calculate the total papayas and mangos separately and then add them together.

- From the papaya trees, each tree produces 10 papayas, and he has 2 papaya trees. Therefore, the total number of papayas is \(10 \times 2 = 20\) papayas.
- From the mango trees, each tree produces 20 mangos, and he has 3 mango trees. Therefore, the total number of mangos is \(20 \times 3 = 60\) mangos.

Now, we add the total number of papayas and mangos to find the total number of fruits:
\[20 \text{ papayas} + 60 \text{ mangos} = 80 \text{ fruits}\]

Therefore, Santino has a total of 80 fruits.
</reasoning>
<answer>
80
</answer>
