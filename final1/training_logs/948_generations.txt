###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Wendi brought home 4 chickens.  After a few days, she brought home enough additional chickens to double the number of chickens she owned.  Then, a neighbor's dog ate one of her chickens.  Finally, Wendi found an additional 4 less than ten chickens and brought them home too.  After this, how many chickens does Wendi have?

#### ANS ####

13
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####


system
<reasoning>
Wendi initially bought 4 chickens.
After a few days, she bought more chickens to double the number she owned, so she bought 4 more chickens, bringing the total to \(4 + 4 = 8\) chickens.
Then, a neighbor's dog ate one chicken, leaving her with \(8 - 1 = 7\) chickens.
Finally, she found an additional 4 less than ten chickens, which means she found 6 more chickens. Adding these to her current count gives \(7 + 6 = 13\) chickens.

Therefore, after these events, Wendi has 13 chickens.
</reasoning>
