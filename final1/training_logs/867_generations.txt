###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

In 2021, Wayne is 37 years old.  His brother Peter is 3 years older than him and their sister Julia is 2 years older than Peter.  What year was Julia born in?

#### ANS ####

1979
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
To determine the year Julia was born, we need to calculate the age of her siblings based on Wayne's age and then find out the birth year of Julia.

1. Wayne is 37 years old in 2021.
2. Wayne's brother Peter is 3 years older than Wayne, so Peter's age in 2021 is \(37 + 3 = 40\) years old.
3. Wayne's sister Julia is 2 years older than Peter, so Julia's age in 2021 is \(40 + 2 = 42\) years old.
4. Julia's age in 2021 is 42 years. If we subtract her age from the current year to find her birth year, we get \(2021 - 42 = 1979\).

Therefore, Julia was born in 1979.
</reasoning>
<answer>
