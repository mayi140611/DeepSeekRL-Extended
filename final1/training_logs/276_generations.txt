###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Sabrina went to the library and found a historical series novel called The Rangers Apprentice. There are 14 books in the series, and each book has 200 pages. She read four books in a month and half the number of books remaining in the second month. What's the total number of pages Sabrina has to read to finish the whole series?

#### ANS ####

1000
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
First, let's calculate the number of books Sabrina has to read from the series she has already read:
14 total books / 4 books already read = 3.5 books. Since it's not possible to read half a book, we'll round down to the nearest whole number.

Now let's calculate the number of books she has left to read:
14 total books - 4 books already read = 10 books remaining.

In the second month, Sabrina reads half the number of books remaining:
10 books remaining / 2 = 5 books.

So in total, Sabrina has read 4 + 5 = 9 books.

To find out how many pages she has read, we multiply the number of books by the number of pages per book:
9 books * 200 pages/book = 1800 pages.

Since the total number of pages in all the books is 14 * 200 = 2800 pages, the number of pages she still has to read is:
2800 pages - 1800 pages = 1000 pages.

</reasoning>

</answer>
