###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Emery and Serena go to their school library, and each borrows a copy of The life of Jack Steve's book to read for their school project. If Emery can read five times as fast as Serena, and the book takes her 20 days to read, what's the average number of days the two take to read the book?

#### ANS ####

60
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####


system
Let's denote the number of days it takes Serena to read the book as \( S \).

Since Emery can read five times as fast as Serena, the time it takes Emery to read the book is \( \frac{S}{5} \).

Given that Emery takes 20 days to read the book, we have:
\[ \frac{S}{5} = 20 \]

Now, we need to solve for \( S \):
\[ S = 20 \times 5 \]
\[ S = 100 \]

So, Serena takes 100 days to read the book.

Now, to find the average number of days the two take to read the book, we add Emery's and Serena's reading times and divide by 2:
\[ \text{Average reading days} = \frac{20 + 100}{2} \]
\[ \text{Average reading days} = \frac{120}{2} \]
\[ \text{Average reading days} = 60 \]

</reasoning>
<answer>
60
</answer>
