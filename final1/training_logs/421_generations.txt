###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Bryan works as a social media account manager. He does marketing posts, advertisement posts, and customer outreach posts. His client has him spend four hours on customer outreach posts and half that time on advertisement posts each day. Bryan works eight hours a day. How much time in hours each day does he spend on marketing posts?

#### ANS ####

2

system
Let's calculate the time Bryan spends on advertisement posts first. Since he spends half the time he spends on customer outreach posts on advertisement posts, and he spends 4 hours on customer outreach posts, he spends 4 / 2 = 2 hours on advertisement posts each day.

Now, let's add up the time he spends on customer outreach and advertisement posts: 4 hours (customer outreach) + 2 hours (advertisement) = 6 hours per day.

Since Bryan works 8 hours a day, the time he spends on marketing posts is the total hours minus the hours spent on customer outreach and advertisement posts: 8 hours (total) - 6 hours (customer outreach and advertisement) = 2 hours per day on marketing posts.
