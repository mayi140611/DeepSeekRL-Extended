###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

In a race, there are eight runners. The first five runners finish the race in 8 hours, while the rest of the runners finish the race 2 hours later. Calculate the total time the eight runners took to finish the race.

#### ANS ####

70
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
To find the total time taken by the eight runners to finish the race, we need to sum the time taken by each group of runners.

The first five runners finish the race in 8 hours. There are 5 such runners.

The remaining three runners finish the race 2 hours later, which means they take \(8 + 2 = 10\) hours each.

So, the total time taken by the first five runners is:
\[5 \times 8 = 40\ \text{hours}.\]

The total time taken by the remaining three runners is:
\[3 \times 10 = 30\ \text{hours}.\]

Therefore, the total time taken by all eight runners is:
\[40 + 30 = 70\ \text{hours}.\]

So, the total time the eight runners took to finish the race is \(70\) hours.

</reasoning>
<answer>
70
</answer>
