###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

Tim gets 6 hours of sleep 2 days in a row.  To make up for it he sleeps 10 hours the next 2 days.  How much sleep did he get?

#### ANS ####

32
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
Tim gets 6 hours of sleep for the first 2 days, which totals:
6 hours/day * 2 days = 12 hours

Then, he sleeps 10 hours for the next 2 days, which totals:
10 hours/day * 2 days = 20 hours

To find the total amount of sleep he got, we add the two amounts:
12 hours + 20 hours = 32 hours

Therefore, Tim got a total of 32 hours of sleep.

</reasoning>
<answer>
32
</answer>
