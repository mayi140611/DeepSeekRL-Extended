###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

During one hour, Tom can read 12 pages of a book. How many pages would he be able to read during 2 hours if he could increase his reading speed by a factor of 3?

#### ANS ####

72
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
Tom's reading speed is 12 pages per hour. If he increases his speed by a factor of 3, his new reading speed would be:
\[ 12 \text{ pages/hour} \times 3 = 36 \text{ pages/hour} \]

If he continues to read for 2 hours at this new speed, the total number of pages he would read would be:
\[ 36 \text{ pages/hour} \times 2 \text{ hours} = 72 \text{ pages} \]

So, the total number of pages he would read is 72.
</reasoning>
<answer>
72
</answer>
