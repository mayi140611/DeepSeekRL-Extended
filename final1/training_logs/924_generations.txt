###### ORIGINAL PROMPT #####

In a glee club, there are two times as many female than male members. How many female members are there if there are 18 members in the club?

#### ANS ####

12
#### GENERATION 1 RESPONSE ####

system
<reasoning>
Let's denote the number of male members as \( m \). According to the information given, the number of female members is twice the number of male members, so we can express the number of female members as \( 2m \).

The total number of members in the club is given as 18. Therefore, we can set up the following equation:
\[ m + 2m = 18 \]
\[ 3m = 18 \]

Now, we can solve for \( m \):
\[ m = \frac{18}{3} = 6 \]

So, there are 6 male members. Since the number of female members is twice the number of male members, there are:
\[ 2 \times 6 = 12 \]
female members.

Therefore, there are 12 female members in the glee club.
</reasoning>
