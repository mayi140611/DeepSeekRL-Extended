Henrietta is repainting her house. The walls in the living room take up 600 square feet. She has three bedrooms. The walls in each bedroom take up 400 square feet. If one gallon of paint can cover 600 square feet, how many gallons of paint does Henrietta need to paint her house?

#### ANS ####

3

   - Total square footage of bedroom walls: 400 square feet/bedroom * 3 bedrooms = 1200 square feet
   - Total square footage of walls in the living room and bedrooms: 600 square feet + 1200 square feet = 1800 square feet

2. Calculate how many gallons of paint are needed:
   - One gallon of paint covers 600 square feet

   - Number of gallons needed: 1800 square feet / 600 square feet/gallon = 3 gallons
